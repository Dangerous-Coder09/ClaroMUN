About the Committee

Learn what delegates will discuss, research, and practice in UNSC.

The United Nations Security Council committee places delegates at the center of high-stakes diplomacy. Delegates represent member states as they respond to urgent threats to international peace and security through debate, negotiation, and resolution writing.

This committee rewards strategic thinking, strong country research, and practical solutions. Delegates should be prepared to balance national interest with international responsibility while navigating alliances, veto powers, and crisis-driven discussions.
