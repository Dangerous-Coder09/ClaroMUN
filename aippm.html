About the Committee

Learn what delegates will discuss, research, and practice in WHO.

The World Health Organization committee focuses on the intersection of science, public policy, and international cooperation. Delegates discuss global health challenges and work toward solutions that can protect communities across borders.

This committee encourages evidence-based debate, empathy, and practical planning. Delegates should research health systems, international coordination, prevention strategies, and the needs of vulnerable populations.
