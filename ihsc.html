About the Committee

Learn what delegates will discuss, research, and practice in IHSC.

International Historic Security Council: As the world trembles beneath the thunder of war and the fate of nations hangs by a thread, the International Historic Security Council transports delegates to an age where every decision carries the weight of history. Set against the backdrop of the Second World War at one of its most pivotal moments, this committee places delegates as representatives of nations caught in a struggle that will determine the future of the world. Entrusted with safeguarding their countries' interests, delegates must navigate the intricate realities of wartime diplomacy, military strategy, and international cooperation. From military campaigns and territorial disputes to economic warfare and political negotiations, delegates must confront challenges that extend far beyond the front lines. In this simulation, uncertainty is constant. Intelligence is incomplete, loyalties are fragile, and the consequences of miscalculation can be catastrophic. As nations compete, cooperate, and struggle for survival, delegates must determine not only how the war will be fought, but also what kind of world will emerge from its aftermath. The fate of nations—and perhaps humanity itself—rests in the hands of the International Historic Security Council
