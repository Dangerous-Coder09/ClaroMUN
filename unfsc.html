The Gathering Storm

United Nations Futuristic Security Council

At the Crossroads of War and Destiny

About the Committee

Learn what delegates will discuss, research, and practice in UNFSC.

The year is 2058. What began as a routine audit has spiraled into the greatest crisis of the modern era. A series of classified leaks has exposed decades of manipulated resource data, revealing that the world's reserves of food, freshwater, energy resources, and strategic minerals are far lower than governments, corporations, and international institutions had claimed. Overnight, markets have collapsed, supply chains have faltered, and confidence in the global order has evaporated. As panic spreads across nations, accusations of corruption, secret stockpiling, data manipulation, and institutional negligence dominate international discourse. States find themselves grappling with shortages, economic instability, civil unrest, and mounting pressure to secure their own survival in an increasingly uncertain world. In this emergency session of the United Nations Futuristic Security Council (UNFSC) delegates will navigate a dynamic and rapidly evolving crisis featuring intelligence leaks, diplomatic betrayals, covert negotiations, emerging power blocs, resource disputes, economic warfare, infrastructure crises, and escalating geopolitical tensions. With trust in international institutions at an all-time low and every nation facing difficult choices between cooperation and self-preservation, delegates must determine accountability, rebuild confidence in global governance, secure access to critical resources, and prevent the world from descending into an era of resource-driven conflict. In a world where every state has something to lose, something to hide, and something to fight for, the decisions made by this council may determine whether humanity enters a new age of cooperation—or the first great conflict of the resource age.
