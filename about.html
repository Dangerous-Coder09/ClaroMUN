About ClaroMUN

A platform where student diplomacy meets purpose, preparation, and leadership.

ClaroMUN is designed to bring delegates together for thoughtful debate, global awareness, and confident public speaking in a professional conference environment.

Who We Are

ClaroMUN is a school-based Model United Nations conference built to inspire research, negotiation, and collaboration.

Our Mission

We aim to nurture articulate, informed, and empathetic student leaders who can engage with pressing global issues through respectful diplomacy.

What Delegates Gain

Delegates develop public speaking, policy research, drafting, teamwork, and consensus-building skills while representing their assigned countries.

Conference Experience

From opening ceremony to committee sessions, ClaroMUN offers a structured and memorable experience that balances academic challenge with student leadership.

Words of the Secretary-General

A message to all delegates and guests of ClaroMUN.

Secretary-General's Message

Welcome to ClaroMUN. Our conference is more than a simulation of the United Nations — it is a space for students to become thoughtful speakers, careful listeners, and responsible global citizens. We hope each delegate enters committee with curiosity, confidence, and a willingness to collaborate.

Whether you are a first-time delegate or an experienced participant, this conference is an opportunity to challenge yourself, engage with diverse ideas, and grow as a leader. We are proud to host you, and we hope your ClaroMUN experience is both enriching and unforgettable.
