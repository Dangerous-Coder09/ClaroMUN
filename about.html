About ClaroMUN

A platform where student diplomacy meets purpose, preparation, and leadership.

ClaroMUN is designed to bring delegates together for thoughtful debate, global awareness, and confident public speaking in a professional conference environment.

Who We Are

ClaroMUN is a school-based Model United Nations conference built to inspire research, negotiation, and collaboration.

Our Mission

We aim to nurture articulate, informed, and empathetic student leaders who can engage with pressing global issues through respectful diplomacy.

What Delegates Gain

Delegates develop public speaking, policy research, drafting, teamwork, and consensus-building skills while representing their assigned countries.

Conference Experience

From opening ceremony to committee sessions, ClaroMUN offers a structured and memorable experience that balances academic challenge with student leadership.

Words of the Secretary-General

A message to all delegates and guests of ClaroMUN.

Secretary-General's Message

Three years ago, ClaroMUN was an idea, a hopeful experiment, a room full of nervous delegates who weren't entirely sure what they were doing. (Frankly, neither were we.)

And yet, here we are.

The 3rd Annual Session of ClaroMUN 2026 is not just a milestone, it's proof that something real was built here. What started as a vision has grown into a conference that delegates look forward to, prepare for, and (if we're doing our job right) leave transformed by.

MUN, at its core, is not about winning gavels or sounding impressive in a committee room. It's about learning to sit with complexity, to argue with empathy, and to find common ground with people who see the world entirely differently from you. These are not just diplomatic skills. They are life skills. And ClaroMUN has been a home for that learning since day one.

To our returning delegates, WELCOME back. You already know what this is about.

To those joining us for the first time, you have no idea what you've walked into. In the best possible way.

Let's make this edition the best one yet.

Aditya Banik
Secretary-General, ClaroMUN 2026
