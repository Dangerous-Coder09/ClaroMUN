About the Committee

Learn what delegates will discuss, research, and practice in UNHRC.

The United Nations Human Rights Council committee challenges delegates to approach complex rights issues with clarity, compassion, and principled argumentation. Delegates examine international norms and propose actions to protect human dignity.

This committee values balanced debate, legal awareness, and respectful diplomacy. Delegates should prepare to discuss enforcement, accountability, humanitarian concerns, and realistic cooperation among states.
